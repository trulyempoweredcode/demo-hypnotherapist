Hypnotherapy combines talking therapy with guided relaxation (trance). During trance, your mind enters a focused, calm state where it's more receptive to positive suggestions and new ways of thinking. It's not about being "put under" or losing control — you remain fully aware throughout. Solution-focused hypnotherapy specifically works by helping you imagine your preferred future, which encourages the brain to create new, positive neural pathways.
