This isn't stage hypnosis. It's clinical neuroscience.

Solution-focused hypnotherapy combines the latest understanding of neuroscience with the therapeutic power of trance — a natural, relaxed state of focused attention. During our sessions, we won't dwell on problems or dig through painful memories. Instead, we'll focus entirely on where you want to be and how to get there. It's a positive, forward-looking approach that produces measurable results, often in fewer sessions than traditional talking therapies.
