About Me

Hello, I'm David

I'm a clinical hypnotherapist based in Clifton, Bristol, and I've spent the last twelve years helping people make changes they once thought were impossible. From anxiety and phobias to smoking and sleep problems, I use solution-focused hypnotherapy to help my clients move forward quickly and with confidence.

Before training as a hypnotherapist, I worked in education — teaching psychology at sixth form level. That background in how the mind works is central to my practice. I don't just guide you through trance; I explain the neuroscience behind what's happening, so you understand exactly why it works.

Outside the consulting room, I'm a keen fell runner, an enthusiastic but unreliable cook, and I'm slowly working my way through every independent coffee shop in Bristol.

My Journey

From the Classroom to the Consulting Room

Teaching psychology gave me the theory. Hypnotherapy gave me the tools to apply it.

For ten years I taught A-level psychology, fascinated by how the brain creates patterns of thought and behaviour. But I kept meeting students — and colleagues — who were stuck in cycles of anxiety, self-doubt, and unhelpful habits. The academic understanding was there, but the practical tools to help people change were limited.

That's when I discovered solution-focused hypnotherapy. I trained at the Clifton Practice in Bristol, completed my Hypnotherapy Practitioner Diploma, and began seeing clients in the evenings alongside teaching. Within a year, the results were so consistent that I made the leap to full-time practice. Twelve years later, I've helped over a thousand people reclaim control of their minds — and I'm still as passionate about it as day one.

My Approach

What Makes My Practice Different

These principles shape every session and every client relationship.

1

Solution-Focused

We don't spend time analysing problems or revisiting painful memories. Every session is focused on where you want to be and how to get there. This makes the process positive, empowering, and efficient.

2

Science-Backed

I explain the neuroscience behind every technique so you understand exactly what's happening in your brain. Knowledge is power — and understanding the process makes it even more effective.

3

No Judgement

Whatever you're dealing with, you won't be judged. My consulting room is a completely safe space where you can be honest about what's going on without fear of criticism.

4

Efficient Results

Most clients see significant improvement within 6–8 sessions. Some issues, like smoking, can be resolved in a single session. I'm not interested in keeping you coming back longer than you need to.
