About Me

Hello, I'm James

I'm a qualified clinical hypnotherapist registered with the AfSFH (Association for Solution Focused Hypnotherapy). Based in Bristol for over a decade, I've helped hundreds of people take back control of their lives — whether that's overcoming deep-seated anxiety, breaking free from unwanted habits, or building the confidence to pursue their goals.

My training is rooted in solution-focused psychotherapy combined with clinical hypnosis. I draw on neuroscience and positive psychology to help clients move forward without dwelling on past problems. Every session is collaborative, forward-looking, and tailored to the individual.

Outside the therapy room, I'm an avid cyclist and can often be found exploring the trails around the Bristol area. I believe that a practitioner who looks after their own wellbeing is better placed to help others do the same.

My Journey

How I Got Here

A turning point in my own life showed me the remarkable power of the mind — and set me on a path I've never looked back from.

Several years ago, I went through a particularly difficult period — the kind that leaves you feeling stuck, anxious, and unsure of the way forward. A friend suggested hypnotherapy, and although I was sceptical, I decided I had nothing to lose. The results were extraordinary. Within weeks, I felt calmer, clearer, and more in control than I had in years. I was fascinated by how the process worked and why it had been so effective.

That experience changed the course of my career. I enrolled in clinical hypnotherapy training, completed my Hypnotherapy Practitioner Diploma at CPHT Bristol, and began seeing clients alongside my previous work. The results were so consistent and so rewarding that I soon made the leap to full-time practice. Today, helping people unlock their potential is not just my profession — it's my vocation.
