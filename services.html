Services

Solution-focused hypnotherapy can help with a wide range of issues. Explore the areas where my clients see the most significant, lasting results.

Anxiety & Stress

Anxiety is one of the most common reasons people come to see me. Whether it's a constant background hum of worry, sudden panic attacks, or an inability to switch off at night, solution-focused hypnotherapy can help your brain break free from the anxiety cycle.

Together, we'll work on reducing your stress response, building new neural pathways, and giving you practical tools to manage anxious thoughts. Most clients notice a significant shift within the first few sessions.

  • Generalised anxiety disorder
  • Panic attacks
  • Social anxiety
  • Work-related stress and burnout
  • Health anxiety

Phobias & Fears

Phobias are learned responses — and what your brain has learned, it can unlearn. Whether you're terrified of flying, needles, spiders, or public speaking, hypnotherapy works by gently retraining your brain's response to the trigger.

Using a combination of relaxation techniques and guided visualisation, we'll help your subconscious mind process the fear differently. Many phobias can be significantly reduced or eliminated in just three to four sessions.

  • Fear of flying
  • Needle and dental phobias
  • Public speaking anxiety
  • Animal and insect phobias
  • Driving and travel fears

Smoking Cessation

My smoking cessation programme is designed to help you quit in a single two-hour session. Rather than relying on willpower alone, we work with your subconscious mind to change the way you think about cigarettes at a fundamental level.

Before your session, I'll ask you to prepare mentally for the change. During the session, we'll address the psychological triggers, habits, and associations that keep you reaching for a cigarette — and replace them with healthier patterns.

  • Single-session programme
  • Targets unconscious habits
  • No patches or substitutes needed
  • Includes take-home audio
  • Free follow-up support

Weight Management

Diets work on willpower. Hypnotherapy works on the underlying patterns that drive overeating, emotional eating, and unhealthy food choices. By changing your relationship with food at a subconscious level, lasting change becomes natural rather than forced.

Over a course of sessions, we'll address the triggers and habits behind your eating patterns, build a healthier mindset around food and exercise, and help you develop the motivation and self-belief to maintain your progress long term.

  • Emotional and comfort eating
  • Portion control and mindful eating
  • Sugar and snack cravings
  • Building exercise motivation
  • Long-term habit change

Confidence & Self-Esteem

Low confidence and poor self-esteem affect every area of life — from relationships and career progression to daily interactions and personal fulfilment. Hypnotherapy helps by working directly with the subconscious beliefs that hold you back.

Through our sessions, we'll challenge the negative self-talk, build a stronger sense of self-worth, and help you develop the inner confidence to pursue the things that matter to you. The changes often feel surprisingly natural and lasting.

  • Imposter syndrome
  • Performance anxiety
  • Self-worth and self-belief
  • Assertiveness and boundaries
  • Negative self-talk patterns

Sleep & Insomnia

Poor sleep affects everything — your mood, your energy, your ability to think clearly, and your physical health. If you're lying awake at night with a racing mind, waking in the early hours, or simply never feeling rested, hypnotherapy can help reset your sleep patterns.

We'll work on reducing the anxiety and overthinking that disrupt sleep, establishing healthier sleep habits, and using trance to train your brain to switch off naturally. Many clients report better sleep within the first two or three sessions.

  • Difficulty falling asleep
  • Waking during the night
  • Racing thoughts at bedtime
  • Anxiety-related insomnia
  • Restoring natural sleep cycles
